Ask Chopper Music Production Guide

Introduction:
Ask Chopper is an AI-powered music assistant designed to help producers, musicians, and creators with their music production questions and workflow optimization.

Key Features:
1. Beat Pax - A marketplace for sharing and discovering audio packs
2. Sound library with categorized content (Beats, Sound Pax, Music)
3. Token-based system for accessing premium content
4. Real-time chat assistance for music production questions

Production Tips:
- Always start with setting the right BPM (Beats Per Minute) for your track
- Use the correct musical key to ensure harmonic consistency
- Layer sounds carefully to avoid frequency clashing
- Export in high-quality formats (WAV 24-bit recommended)

Genre Support:
Ask Chopper supports various genres including:
- Hip Hop
- Afrobeats
- Electronic
- Jazz
- R&B
- Pop

Token System:
- Listening to a track: 1 token
- Downloading a track: 3 tokens
- Users can purchase tokens or earn them through uploads

Community Features:
Users can upload their own audio packs, track downloads, and interact with other creators. The platform includes user profiles, activity tracking, and feedback systems.

Contact:
For support and feedback, use the feedback form in the application menu.
